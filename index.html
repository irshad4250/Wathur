Weather from Mars

The measurements are being taken by Nasa's Curiosity rover at Gale Crater in the southern Hemisphere of Mars. All the informations come from an API, and latest data is at least 2 days back from current day.

To know:

  • You can search for a particular Sol (Sol is a solar day on Mars).
  • The maximum sol date will always be shown when website is first loaded.
  • You can reset the measurements to maximum sol.
